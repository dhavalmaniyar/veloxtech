ABOUT VELOXTECH SOLUTIONS.


To put it simply, we identify and work closely on IT Infrastructure , Analytics and professional services with OEM's to identify the Pain Points of the client to deliver results for their clients. We do this through a process that melds the best of traditional IT approach and newer trend recognition through predictive analytics. Our Proof of concepts helps start-ups, mid-market and large enterprises find partners that meet their needs, whether for a one-off project or a long term relationship.


OUR STORY


VeloxTech Solutions was created to solve an important, two sided problem. How do buyers of IT Infrastructure, professional services and software find the best Original equipment companies to meet a specific need? And conversely, how do leading Product, services and software firms stand out from the sea of mediocre competitors? As a buyer with a large budget, one option is to hire a Team for research to identify even expensive, OEM standard solutions and practices without prediction of Future Growth V/s ROI. But what if you wanted to work with leading specialists at more nimble, focused companies or had a limited budget? VeloxTech Solutions was born in 2015 to help solve this problem. Our founders had firsthand experience as both buyers, solution architects and sellers of solutions and software and knew there must be a better way. We looked to both the large, traditional IT research firms and more Disruptive customers for inspiration and developed a new approach that included the best of both worlds.
